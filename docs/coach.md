# 🧠 Optimization coach

One screen that tells you what to do next — all computed locally from your save.

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| 🎯 **Overview** | Your single best next move, an "also worth doing" to-do list, and party DPS / POWER / gold / stage at a glance. |
| 👋 **Return Briefing** | Reopen the app after a break and get a recap: gold waiting to claim, chests that overflowed while you were away, and your deltas since last visit. (A plain reload doesn't count as "away".) |
| 💡 **Insight strip** | Up to three blunt cards on the Overview: gold left on the table by farming the wrong stage, active-vs-idle, and your next level-up ETA — each jumps to the right tab. |
