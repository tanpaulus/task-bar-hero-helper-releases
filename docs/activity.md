# ⏱ Activity & history

Timers and your real run history — so you know when to come back and how you're trending.

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| ⏳ **Idle timer** | Progress toward the offline-reward cap and the best stage to park at. |
| 📦 **Chest planner** | Auto-open cooldowns and capacity per tier, a best-stage-to-fill plan, and a yield tracker across your runs. |
| 📜 **Runs history** | Every cleared run (stage, team, clear time, DPS, gold, EXP, drops, deaths) grouped into sessions — with refresh & reset. |
| 📊 **History** | Your POWER, gold **and stash value (net worth)** charted over time, plus a **7-day change** on your stash value. |

!!! info "Where run history comes from"
    Runs are logged automatically while the optional **live DPS overlay** is on. See [In-game overlays](overlays.md).
