# 🖥 In-game overlays

Two optional overlays that sit on top of the game. **Both are off by default** and only in the Windows desktop app.

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| 💲 **Price HUD** | A transparent price card beside the item you hover in-game: instant-sell, suggested list, deal tag, sparkline and sell-now score. |
| ⚡ **Live DPS overlay** | Always-on-top stage / DPS / damage / gold / EXP with a live time-to-level bar — and it logs your runs for the [Runs history](activity.md). |

!!! success "Read-only by design"
    The overlays read the game's memory **read-only** — no writes, no injection, no network from the reader. The small reader component is **downloaded and verified (SHA-256) on first enable**, and everything stays off until you turn it on in Settings.
